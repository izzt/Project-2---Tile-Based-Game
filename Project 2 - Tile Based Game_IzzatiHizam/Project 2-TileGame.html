Help Harry the Astronaut get to the Moon!

How to play:

Use the arrow keys to move move my space ship up, down, left, or right.
1. Spaceship - Use it to buy food for Harry.
2. Alien - Fight aliens. you might get their gold or they might get yours.
3. Monster - Avoid at all cost.
